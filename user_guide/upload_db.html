Uploading of database to a localhost

Author : Judywen Guapin

PHP version: 5.6.21

  • 1. Locatate the ".sql" file inside of user guide folder and ready it for the upload later.

  • 2. On your browser, type "localhost/phpmyadmin".


  • 3. Click databases (#1 :: refer in image), (#2) create desired database name, (#3) click create to create your database.


  • 4. Find and click your newly created databaseName at the leftside (#1). Click import to import your database (#2).


  • 5. Click the browse button (#1) and then locate the ".sql" file the you already prepare then click open (#2).


  • 6. At the bottom click go button then wait for the localhost to upload your database. That's all. Happy coding!.
